Cake donuts are another popular type of doughnut that get their rise from baking powder. They are denser than yeast donuts because of the cake batter that makes them up. You'll also notice they have a drier texture with more crumbs. A cake donut can come in a wide range of flavors, from vanilla to chocolate. They can be topped with either powdered sugar or cinnamon sugar.
